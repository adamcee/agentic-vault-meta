# Idea: Add Protocol Explanations to Homepage

## Context
The homepage currently explains the structural benefits of the vault (SYSTEM.md primacy, Decision Records) and how it solves "Amnesia." However, it does not explicitly sell the profound behavioral benefits of the Vault's writing disciplines and protocols.

## The Proposal
Update the homepage (and potentially add a dedicated methodology page) to explicitly explain how the specific writing protocols in the Agentic Vault actively combat negative AI behaviors.

Specifically, map the protocols to the problems they solve:
- **Combating AI Slop:** Explain how the "AI Silence" and "Paragraph Ban" protocols force the AI to strip out "Delve," "Synergy," and excessive throat-clearing, resulting in high-signal, dense output.
- **Combating AI Sycophancy:** Explain how the "Contestable Model" and "Research Traceability" protocols force the AI to ground its answers in recorded decisions and research, rather than just agreeing with whatever the user just said.
- **Combating Cognitive Dependency (AI Dependency Syndrome):** Explain how the vault forces the user to actively manage the SSOT (`SYSTEM.md`) and author ADRs. The user remains the architect; the AI remains the executor. This prevents the user from outsourcing their executive function to the LLM.

## Next Steps
- Draft a new section for `website/docs/index.md` titled "Combating AI Anti-Patterns".
- Ensure the `vault-guide/style-guides/ai-writing-discipline-core.md` explicitly mentions these three anti-patterns as the reason for the rules.
